The Beginning

A Passion for Petals

BloomWire started in 2023 as a small hobby project by Priya, a textile design graduate from Surat. What began as making pipe-cleaner roses for friends quickly turned into an Instagram sensation.

Today, we're a team of five artisans who handcraft every single flower with meticulous attention to detail. We believe that beautiful things should last — and our flowers do exactly that.

Every petal is individually shaped, every stem is wrapped by hand, and every bouquet is arranged with the kind of care that only comes from genuine passion.

The Craft

How We Make Them

Every flower goes through a careful 4-step artisan process.

Design & Sketch

Each flower starts as a hand-drawn sketch, studying real blooms for natural proportions and details.

Shape & Twist

Premium pipe cleaners are carefully twisted and shaped into petals, leaves, and stems with precision tools.

Assemble & Style

Individual components are assembled into complete flowers and styled for a natural, lifelike appearance.

Quality & Pack

Every piece is inspected for quality, then carefully packaged in eco-friendly materials ready for delivery.
